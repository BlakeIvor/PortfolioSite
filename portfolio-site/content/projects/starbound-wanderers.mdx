## Project Overview

A captivating top-down isometric narrative adventure game developed in Unity, featuring sophisticated AI systems, optimized performance solutions, and engaging combat mechanics. Players embark on an epic journey through diverse environments, encountering complex characters and challenging encounters. Successfully demonstrated at PAX East 2023, showcasing its quality and innovation to thousands of attendees and industry professionals.

## Key Features

- **Advanced AI Systems**: Custom A* pathfinding algorithm for intelligent NPC navigation through complex terrain
- **Dynamic Enemy AI**: State-based AI systems that adapt to player behavior and environmental conditions
- **Intuitive Inventory System**: Drag-and-drop inventory management with smart categorization and user-friendly interface
- **Responsive UI Framework**: Scalable interface that adapts to different screen resolutions and input methods
- **Rich Narrative Experience**: Immersive storytelling combined with strategic gameplay in beautifully crafted isometric environments

## Technical Challenges

- **Performance Optimization**: Implementing advanced threading systems (queue & lock systems) to distribute computational load across multiple frames
- **AI Pathfinding**: Developing efficient A* algorithms that handle complex terrain and dynamic obstacles in real-time
- **Memory Management**: Creating efficient object pooling and garbage collection optimization to maintain consistent frame rates
- **State Management**: Building sophisticated behavior trees for NPCs and enemies with complex decision-making systems
- **Collaborative Development**: Managing large-scale Unity project with PlasticSCM version control across multidisciplinary team
- **Cross-Platform Compatibility**: Ensuring consistent performance and user experience across different hardware configurations

The project successfully demonstrates advanced game development techniques while delivering an engaging player experience. The PAX East showcase validated the game's market appeal and technical excellence, representing a significant achievement in collaborative game development.
